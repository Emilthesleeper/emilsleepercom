BBS Movie Studio is a compact film modpack centered around the BBS mod by McHorse, with which you can create complex scenes, animations, and complete films in Minecraft. Thanks to Blockbench model support, finely adjustable movements, and numerous pre-made BBS assets, you can recreate almost any scene and animate it precisely.
